Our Commitment to Sustainability

Outer is deeply committed to reducing its environmental impact by using sustainable practices in both design and production. We integrate recycled materials into our furniture designs, minimize waste, and ensure energy-efficient production processes.

The company's dedication to eco-consciousness begins with selecting materials that not only perform well but are also sourced responsibly. By integrating recycled materials into their furniture designs, Outer minimizes waste and reduces reliance on non-renewable resources.

Our commitment to sustainability extends to packaging as well, where recyclable and biodegradable materials are used to ship our products safely to customers.

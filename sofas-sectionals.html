Transforming Outdoor Spaces

Outer's outdoor sofas and sectionals are designed to redefine how we interact with our outdoor spaces. A comfortable, stylish sectional can easily become the centerpiece of a patio, deck, or garden, encouraging relaxation, socializing, and even dining al fresco.

Whether you're hosting a summer barbecue, enjoying a quiet evening under the stars, or simply lounging in the sun, Outer's furniture enables you to extend your living space, transforming your outdoor area into a luxurious retreat.

Innovative Features

OuterShell™ Technology

Our patented OuterShell™ technology acts as a protective "coat" for the furniture, shielding cushions and frames from rain, dirt, and debris when not in use. This innovative cover enhances longevity and reduces the need for constant cleaning and maintenance.

Modular Design

Outer's sectionals offer versatile configurations to fit any space. Start with a few pieces and expand or rearrange as needed, adapting to changing outdoor activities from intimate gatherings to larger parties.

Care and Maintenance Tips

Use OuterShell™

Always cover your furniture when not in use to protect from UV damage and debris.

Regular Cleaning

Wipe cushions with a damp cloth and clean frames with mild soap and water.

Extreme Weather

Store cushions indoors during prolonged rain or snow to extend fabric life.

Frame Inspection

Periodically check aluminum frames for wear and touch up if needed.

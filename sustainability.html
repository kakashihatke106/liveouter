The Importance of Sustainable Outdoor Furniture

Sustainable outdoor furniture plays a crucial role in creating environmentally conscious lifestyles. By choosing eco-friendly options, we can reduce waste, minimize carbon footprints, and support long-term ecological balance.

Key benefits of sustainable outdoor furniture include:

  • Increased durability and longevity
  • Reduction of carbon footprint
  • Creation of healthier living environments
  • Support for ethical manufacturing practices
  • Aesthetic appeal combined with eco-consciousness

Eco-Friendly Materials in Modern Outdoor Furniture

Recycled Materials

We use recycled plastics, aluminum, and wood to reduce waste and energy consumption in manufacturing.

Sustainably Sourced Wood

Our wood comes from responsibly managed forests, ensuring biodiversity and replenishment.

Marine-Grade Fabrics

We use solution-dyed acrylic and recycled polyester fabrics that are durable and weather-resistant.

Sustainable Manufacturing Processes

Energy Efficiency

We use renewable energy sources and energy-efficient machinery to reduce our carbon footprint.

Waste Reduction

Our designs minimize material waste, and we recycle or repurpose leftover materials.

Local Sourcing

We source materials locally to reduce transportation-related emissions and support local economies.

Minimalist Packaging

We use recyclable materials and minimize packaging to reduce waste.

How Outer Incorporates Sustainability

At Outer, sustainability is at the core of our design and manufacturing process. We focus on producing high-quality, eco-friendly furniture that is both stylish and durable.

  • Use of recycled materials like plastic bottles and post-consumer plastic waste
  • Solution-dyed fabric made from recycled polyester for cushions
  • FSC-certified wood for frames
  • Marine-grade stainless steel for durability
  • Circular design philosophy for easy part replacement
  • "Buy Back" program for responsible recycling or repurposing
Our Sustainability Goals
  • Reduce carbon footprint in production
  • Increase use of recycled materials
  • Implement eco-friendly packaging solutions
  • Support sustainable forestry practices
  • Promote circular economy through product design and buy-back programs

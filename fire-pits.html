Maintenance and Safe Operation Tips

  1. Regular Cleaning: Clean the burner and ignition system to prevent buildup of dirt or debris.
  2. Cover When Not in Use: Protect your fire pit from outdoor elements with custom-fit covers.
  3. Proper Ventilation: Place your fire pit in an open area with plenty of ventilation.
  4. Fuel Maintenance: Ensure propane or natural gas tanks are full and securely connected.
  5. Safe Operation: Never leave the fire pit unattended and follow all safety instructions in the product manual.

Elevate Your Outdoor Dining Experience

Durable and Elegant Dining Sets for Every Space

Outer's dining sets are designed to bring the comfort and elegance of indoor dining to your outdoor space. Whether you're hosting a casual brunch or an elegant dinner party, our weather-resistant tables and chairs provide the perfect setting for memorable meals.

The Appeal of Al Fresco Dining

There's something magical about dining outdoors. Whether you're in a cozy courtyard, on a spacious deck, or even in a lush garden, the open air brings an element of relaxation and enjoyment that indoor dining can't replicate. Al fresco dining encourages slower, more mindful meals, and enhances the overall experience by connecting you with your surroundings.

Material Choices for Lasting Durability

Aluminum

Many of Outer's dining sets feature high-quality aluminum frames, which are lightweight, rust-resistant, and incredibly durable. Aluminum is an excellent choice for outdoor furniture because it doesn't corrode or weaken over time, even when exposed to rain, humidity, or salty air.

Teak Wood

Outer's dining tables often incorporate teak wood, a material renowned for its natural beauty and exceptional durability. Teak has a high oil content, making it resistant to water, rot, and insects. It's perfect for outdoor use, with a timeless, elegant look that blends seamlessly with various styles of outdoor décor.

Weather-Resistant Fabric

For seating, Outer's dining sets feature cushions made from performance fabrics that are resistant to water, stains, and UV damage. These cushions offer a comfortable dining experience while withstanding the rigors of outdoor use.

Concrete and Ceramic

Some of Outer's more contemporary dining tables feature concrete or ceramic surfaces. These materials are highly durable, resistant to scratches, and easy to maintain. They also bring a modern, industrial touch to outdoor dining areas.

Combining Style with Functionality

Comfort and Seating Design

Outer's dining sets feature generous cushions with high-density foam, providing support for long meals and conversations. The cushions are wrapped in performance fabric that resists fading, staining, and mildew, making them as practical as they are comfortable.

Design Features and Aesthetics

From sleek, modern designs to more traditional, rustic looks, Outer's dining sets come in a variety of design aesthetics. Features include sleek lines and minimalist frames, rustic elegance with teak wood and wrought iron, and adjustable configurations for flexibility.

Weather Resistance and Easy Maintenance

  • Sun and UV Resistance: Fabrics resist fading from prolonged sun exposure.
  • Rain and Water Resistance: High-quality coatings and treated surfaces protect against water damage.
  • Easy to Clean: Most materials can be easily wiped down or hosed off to remove dirt and debris.

Tips for Choosing the Right Dining Set

1. Size and Space

Consider how many people you typically entertain outdoors and how much space you have available.

2. Style and Aesthetic

Choose a set that complements the rest of your outdoor décor, whether contemporary or rustic.

3. Functionality

Look for features like adjustable tables, comfortable seating, and weather-resistant cushions.

4. Durability and Maintenance

Prioritize materials that can withstand sun, rain, and wind, especially in harsh weather conditions.
